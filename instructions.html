How to play

Rules

The goal of this game is to definitly kill your opponent. Each cat (player) has 9 lives. The cats run on a circuit made of pipes, and can't get out of it. When a cat collides with something, it looses one life. If both cats hit each over, they both loose one life.

Items description

Ice Pick
The ice pick makes a hole in the pipe, leaving a fountain behind the cat. A cat who hits the fountain looses one life, and corks the hole.
Wool Ball
The wool ball acts like a missile. It follows the pipes, choosing a random direction at each intersection. A cat hit by the wool ball looses one life.
Nyan rainbow!
A cat who uses the Nyan rainbow! goes faster, plays the Nyan cat song, and shits a rainbow for a limited time. Use it to hit the other cat.
